Email Not Sending? Here’s How to Fix It

If your emails are not sending, you’re not alone. This is one of the most common issues with email platforms, funnels, and autoresponders.

Common Reasons Emails Fail

  • Domain not authenticated (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • Sending IP flagged or throttled
  • Email provider blocking automated sends
  • Incorrect SMTP or API settings

Quick Fix Checklist

  1. Verify your sending domain
  2. Check spam and bounce logs
  3. Test with a plain-text email
  4. Reduce sending volume temporarily
